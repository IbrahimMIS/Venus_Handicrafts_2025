leadership

Led by Mr. Muhammad Hassan, a visionary entrepreneur with a deep understanding of traditional craftsmanship, Vinus Craft continues to innovate in the world of home decor. Her leadership ensures that each handcrafted item reflects beauty, culture, and quality.

Handicrafts Manufacturer with 36+ Years of Expertise

We specialize in a wide range of handcrafted products, created by skilled artisans and craftsmen. With over 36 years of experience in the industry, our products stand out in the market for their superior quality, elegant design, and fine craftsmanship.
Each item is a classic example of traditional artistry blended with contemporary style. Our collection includes unique pieces that enhance spaces with both charm and character.

Customized Handicraft Solutions

We understand that every customer is unique, and so are their requirements. That’s why we offer customization options for design, size, and color. Whether you’re looking for something traditional or wish to bring your own vision to life, our dedicated design team is here to collaborate with you to craft personalized masterpieces.
