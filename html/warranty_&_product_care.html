Product Care Guidelines

To ensure the beauty and longevity of your handcrafted products, we recommend the following care instructions:

For Wooden Furniture:

  • Clean with a soft, dry cloth. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture to prevent fading or warping.
  • Use coasters or mats to protect surfaces from hot items or spills.

For Metal Items:

  • Wipe regularly with a dry or slightly damp cloth.
  • Avoid exposure to water or humidity for extended periods to prevent rusting.
  • Apply a light coat of clear furniture wax if needed for extra shine and protection.

For Decor Items:

  • Dust regularly using a microfiber cloth.
  • Handle with care to avoid chips or cracks in intricate designs.

By following these simple care instructions, you can maintain the charm and durability of your Vinus Craft products for years to come.
